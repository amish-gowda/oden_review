My Youth Romantic Comedy is Wrong, As I Expected

A Witty and Thought-Provoking High School Comedy
Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

My Youth Romantic Comedy Is Wrong, As I Expected (also known as "Oregairu") is a delightful anime series that skillfully blends humor, romance, and introspection to offer a unique perspective on the complexities of high school life. With its witty dialogue, relatable characters, and nuanced exploration of youth, Oregairu stands out as a refreshing and thought-provoking addition to the genre of romantic comedies.

Arcs:

  1. Everyday Life arc
  2. Cultural Festival arc
  3. School Trip arc
  4. Summer Camp arc
  5. Christmas arc
  6. Valentine's Day arc
  7. Prom arc
  8. Graduation arc

Each of these arcs explores different aspects of the characters' lives, relationships, and personal growth as they navigate the challenges of high school and the complexities of their emotions.
